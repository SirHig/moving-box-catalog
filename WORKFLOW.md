# Moving Day Workflow Guide

## Complete Moving Box Catalog Process

### Phase 1: Before You Start Packing (5 minutes)

**Goal**: Generate and print all your box labels at once

1. **Open the app** (or visit your deployed URL)
2. **Tap "🏷️ Generate Labels"**
3. **Select label template**:
   - Avery 5163 (2" x 4") - Best for boxes ✅
   - Avery 5161 (1" x 4") - Smaller
   - Avery 5160 (1" x 2-5/8") - Smallest
4. **Set quantity**: 
   - Estimate how many boxes you'll need (e.g., 1-50)
   - You can always generate more later
5. **Download PDF**
6. **Print**:
   - **Option A**: Print on Avery label sheets (exact template match)
   - **Option B**: Print on regular paper, cut, and tape to boxes
   - **Option C**: Print and use your Niimbot to make nicer labels from the PDF

💡 **Tip**: Print a few extra sheets - you'll likely need more boxes than expected!

---

### Phase 2: While Packing (30 seconds per box)

**Goal**: Quickly catalog each box as you pack it

#### For Each Box:

1. **Apply label** to the box before packing
2. **Start packing** items into the box
3. **Before closing**: Open the app on your phone
4. **Tap "📷 Catalog Box"**
5. **Scan the QR code** on the box
   - Point your phone camera at the QR code
   - App will automatically detect the box number
   - If scanner doesn't work, tap "Enter Box Number Manually"
6. **Take a photo** of the contents (before closing the box)
   - Get a clear shot showing the items
   - Don't worry about perfect framing
7. **Wait ~3 seconds** for AI to analyze
8. **Done!** Box is cataloged

💡 **Pro Tips**:
- Take photos BEFORE closing the box flaps
- Don't need to remove items - just a clear view
- AI works best with well-lit photos
- You can mark boxes as fragile/priority later

---

### Phase 3: Moving Day & Unpacking

**Goal**: Find items quickly without opening every box

#### Finding Items:

1. **Open the app**
2. **Search** for what you need:
   - "coffee maker"
   - "winter coats"
   - "kids toys"
   - "bathroom towels"
3. **Results show** which box number has those items
4. **View photo** to confirm before opening

#### Marking Boxes:

- **Fragile** ⚠️: Tap the warning icon on delicate items
- **Priority** ⭐: Mark "open first" essentials
- **Filter**: View only fragile or priority boxes

## Troubleshooting

### QR Scanner Not Working
- **Solution 1**: Ensure good lighting on the QR code
- **Solution 2**: Hold phone steady for 2 seconds
- **Solution 3**: Tap "Enter Box Number Manually" and type the number

### Camera Won't Open
- **iOS**: Must use Safari (not Chrome)
- **Android**: Allow camera permissions
- **Desktop**: File upload works instead

### AI Description Is Vague
- **Cause**: Photo too dark or items not visible
- **Solution**: Take another photo with better lighting
- **Note**: You can delete and re-catalog if needed

### Box Already Exists
- **Cause**: You already cataloged that box number
- **Solution**: 
  - Check if it was a mistake
  - Delete the old entry if you want to replace it
  - Or use a different label

## Best Practices

✅ **Do**:
- Label boxes before packing
- Take photos with good lighting
- Include a mix of overview + detail shots
- Mark fragile items immediately
- Generate extra labels (you'll need them)

❌ **Don't**:
- Pack items you'll need immediately (first night box)
- Close box before taking photo
- Rely on memory - catalog everything!
- Skip labeling "obvious" boxes (you'll forget)
